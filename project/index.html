Delta Airlines

Perhaps the Strongest US Carrier

Posted by Brenden Bodily on April 27, 2023

Delta Airlines [Ticker: DAL] is a US airline based in Atlanta, Georgia. Founded in 1925, Delta is one of three legacy full-service network passenger air carriers in the United States, along with competitors United Airlines and American Airlines. As of December 2022, Delta operates more than 4,000 daily flights to over 275 global destinations.
